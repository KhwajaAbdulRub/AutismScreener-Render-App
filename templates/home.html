Understanding Autism

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a developmental condition that affects how a person perceives and socializes with others. Early detection and intervention can significantly improve outcomes.

About Our Assessment

Our assessment tool helps identify potential signs of autism by evaluating three key areas:

  • Social Communication

    Assessment of eye contact and non-verbal communication patterns

  • Behavioral Patterns

    Evaluation of repetitive movements and routine preferences

  • Social Interaction

    Analysis of social engagement and relationship building

Why Early Detection Matters

Research shows that early intervention can:
  • Improve communication skills
  • Enhance social interaction abilities
  • Support cognitive development
  • Lead to better long-term outcomes
